What's your history in Web technologies?

Beginnings

Started getting into web design back in 2010 with my high school web teacher(and Wordpress guru) Zac Gordon. There I learned visual design, user interaction, emotions with design, wireframing, prototyping, creating mockups, typography, HTML/CSS/Flash, and all the good stuff of pre-bootstrap design. All before it was bunched up into the looser terms we know today as UI and some bits of UX.

Professional experience

Oct 2014, I got back into mobile responsive design after a seemingly unexpected gig working for a small 4 person startup doing UI and loosely UX work under the title "IT Generalist". There I designed a website for a Veterans Tech Startup where it would be easy for a retired vet or family member to navigate while being visually appealing.

What are you doing currently?

Recently, I brushed off my boots and sprinkled my web roots by enrolling into CareerFoundry, an online based course which taught me mobile responsive design, bootstrap, version control, and building/deploying apps in Rails. Graduated in December 2015.

I currently attend Wyncode a Miami based in-person bootcamp. Check out our pitch day in March.
